Help

How does this work? Unicode homoglyphs (similar looking letters) are used to hide your message.

Your hidden message may only contain these characters: (-bit charset)

If you can't decode your message check that you copied it correctly, and that you didn't copy any extra characters or spaces etc.

This is not encryption software. Anyone who knows to look for your message can find it. It's more like <!-- hidden comments -->.

This software works, but you might see a mangled message or two until I learn which characters Twitter filters/converts. You can also use this for any text (emails, G+, Facebook).
